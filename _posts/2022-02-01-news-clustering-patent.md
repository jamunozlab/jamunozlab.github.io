The patent titled "Entropic clustering of objects" was granted today by the United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO) with number 11,238,308. It describes a method to perform cluster analysis using mathematical graphs and some ideas from thermodynamics and simulated annealing. The owner of this patent is Intel Corporation and the inventor is Jorge Munoz. Check it out [here](https://patents.google.com/patent/US11238308B2/en).
